# Pack: Language Café

**License:** CC BY 4.0 — https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Practice natural language conversations in friendly, everyday settings across
four languages. Build confidence through low-stakes interactions with patient
fictional NPCs who can optionally give gentle language correction.

## Scenarios

| Scenario | Target language | Setting | NPC |
|----------|----------------|---------|-----|
| Coffee at Café Sol | Spanish (es) | Neighbourhood café, Madrid | Rosa García, barista |
| Checking In at Hôtel Lumière | French (fr) | Hotel lobby, Paris | Marie Dupont, receptionist |
| At the Convenience Store | Japanese (ja) | Konbini, Tokyo | Kenji Yamamoto, store clerk |
| Small Talk at the Corner Café | English (en) | Independent café, London | Alex Chen, café regular |

## Correction styles (via difficulty)

Each scenario supports three correction modes selected at the difficulty screen:

| Difficulty | Correction style | What the NPC does |
|------------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Easy | None | Responds naturally without correcting errors |
| Normal (default) | Light | Silently recasts errors using the correct form in the reply |
| Hard | Strict | Points out one error per turn and invites the player to try again |

Correction is always kind and constructive. The debrief reports on
specific communicative skills — never on the player's worth or real-world
language potential.

## Rubric dimensions

All scenarios share a single rubric covering:

- **Comprehension** — did the player understand and respond on-topic?
- **Appropriateness** — was the register and social convention right for the context?
- **Vocabulary Use** — variety and accuracy of vocabulary for the scenario
- **Repair Strategies** — managing misunderstandings (asking for repetition, paraphrasing)
- **Confidence** — communicative initiative and turn-taking

## Content and safety

- Content rating: **G** — suitable for all audiences
- No dating, romantic, or sexual content by default or by design
- All NPCs are fictional adults; no real people impersonated
- Redirect message stays in the NPC's voice to maintain immersion

## Voice readiness

All scenarios are designed for text-only play in this release. Opening lines,
NPC instructions, and player input handling are all UTF-8 safe and tested with
non-English text. Voice input/output integration is a future milestone.
